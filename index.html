🖐 Hand Exercises
📅 Activities
Bridges
  • Lie flat, bend knees, feet flat on bed
  • Push hips up toward ceiling
  • Hold 3 seconds, lower slowly
10 times
Heel Slides
  • Lie flat on your back
  • Slide one heel toward your bottom
  • Slide back out, repeat other side
10 each leg
Leg Lifts
  • Lie flat, tighten one thigh
  • Lift straight leg about 12 inches
  • Hold 3 seconds, lower slowly
10 each leg
Making Fists
  • Open hand wide, fingers straight
  • Slowly make a tight fist
  • Hold 3 seconds, open fully
10 times, 3x daily
Spreading Fingers
  • Place hand flat on a surface
  • Spread fingers as wide as possible
  • Hold 3 seconds, squeeze together
10 times, 3x daily
Finger Rolls
  • Start with fingers straight
  • Slowly curl into a fist, one joint at a time
  • Uncurl back to straight
10 times, 3x daily
